# Opening Checklist

A consistent opening routine ensures the restaurant is ready for service. Complete all tasks in order and sign off when done.

## Arrival & Security (30 min before open)

- [ ] Disarm security system
- [ ] Turn on lights (front and back of house)
- [ ] Check for signs of break-in or damage
- [ ] Verify cash register/safe contents
- [ ] Review manager notes from previous shift

## Safety Systems Check

| System | Verification |
|--------|--------------|
| Fire extinguishers | In place, charged (green zone) |
| Exit signs | All illuminated |
| First aid kit | Stocked and accessible |
| Fire suppression | Armed (hood system) |
| Slip-resistant mats | In position |

## Kitchen Preparation

### Temperature Verification

1. Check walk-in cooler: Must be **40¬∞F (4¬∞C) or below**
2. Check walk-in freezer: Must be **0¬∞F (-18¬∞C) or below**
3. Check prep refrigerators
4. Record all temperatures on log sheet
5. Verify hot holding equipment reaches **140¬∞F (60¬∞C)**

### Equipment Startup

Start equipment in this order:

1. Ovens and grills (15-20 min preheat)
2. Fryers (see Fryer Operation SOP)
3. Steam tables and warmers
4. Coffee machines and beverage equipment
5. Point-of-sale systems

### Prep Station Setup

- [ ] Sanitize all prep surfaces
- [ ] Set up cutting boards by color code
- [ ] Fill sanitizer buckets (test concentration)
- [ ] Stock clean towels and aprons
- [ ] Verify knife kit is complete

## Food Preparation

### Check Prep List

Review prep list for:
- Items needed for today's menu
- Special orders or catering
- Low stock items to prepare
- Items expiring today (use first)

### FIFO Rotation

> **‚ÑπÔ∏è Note**: FIFO = First In, First Out

- Move older items to front of storage
- Check all date labels
- Discard any expired items
- Document discards on waste log

## Front of House

### Dining Area

- [ ] Wipe down all tables and chairs
- [ ] Check booth seating for crumbs/debris
- [ ] Verify table settings complete
- [ ] Ensure salt, pepper, condiments filled
- [ ] Check menu cleanliness

### Restrooms

- [ ] Stock toilet paper and paper towels
- [ ] Fill soap dispensers
- [ ] Clean mirrors and fixtures
- [ ] Empty trash cans
- [ ] Mop floors
- [ ] Verify "Employees must wash hands" sign visible

### Entry & Exterior

- [ ] Sweep entrance and walkways
- [ ] Clean front door glass
- [ ] Check outdoor seating (if applicable)
- [ ] Turn on "OPEN" sign
- [ ] Verify menu boards are accurate

## Staff Preparation

### Uniform & Hygiene Check

All team members must have:
- Clean, pressed uniform
- Name tag visible
- Hair properly restrained
- No jewelry on hands (except plain wedding band)
- No nail polish or artificial nails
- Closed-toe, non-slip shoes

### Pre-Shift Meeting (5 minutes)

Cover the following:
1. Daily specials and 86'd items
2. Reservation count and large parties
3. Staff assignments and breaks
4. Safety reminders
5. Positive note to start the day
