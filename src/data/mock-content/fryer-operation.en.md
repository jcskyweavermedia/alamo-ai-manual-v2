# Fryer Operation

Deep fryers are essential kitchen equipment that require careful operation to ensure food quality and staff safety. Follow all procedures exactly.

## Daily Startup Procedure

Before service, complete these steps in order:

### 1. Safety Check

- Verify fryer is OFF before inspection
- Check oil level (should be between min/max lines)
- Inspect heating elements for damage
- Ensure baskets are clean and undamaged
- Verify fire suppression system is armed

### 2. Oil Quality Check

Perform the following checks before heating:

| Check | Good Oil | Bad Oil - Replace |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| Color | Light golden | Dark brown/black |
| Smell | Neutral/light | Rancid, burnt |
| Clarity | Clear | Cloudy, foamy |
| Smoke | None when heated | Smokes before 350¬∞F |

> **üí° Tip**: Use oil test strips for accurate quality measurement. Replace oil when readings exceed limits.

### 3. Heating

1. Turn fryer ON
2. Set temperature to **350¬∞F (177¬∞C)** for most items
3. Wait 15-20 minutes for oil to reach temperature
4. Verify temperature with external thermometer
5. Oil is ready when a small piece of food bubbles immediately

## Safe Frying Procedures

### Loading the Fryer

- **Never overfill** baskets (fill to 2/3 maximum)
- Lower baskets slowly to prevent splashing
- Stand to the side when lowering baskets
- Keep face away from oil surface

### During Cooking

- Set appropriate timer for each product
- Do not leave fryer unattended
- Monitor oil temperature continuously
- Skim debris from oil surface regularly

### Unloading

- Lift baskets slowly and at an angle
- Allow excess oil to drain (10-15 seconds)
- Transfer to draining rack, not directly to plate
- Never stack hot fried items

## Emergency Procedures

### Oil Fire

> **‚ö†Ô∏è Critical**: NEVER use water on an oil fire. It will cause an explosion.

If a fire occurs:

1. **Turn OFF** the fryer immediately
2. **Cover** with metal lid if small and safe to do so
3. **Activate** fire suppression system if available
4. **Use** Class K fire extinguisher if needed
5. **Evacuate** if fire cannot be controlled
6. **Call 911**

### Oil Spill

1. Turn off fryer and allow oil to cool
2. Block off area to prevent slips
3. Apply absorbent material
4. Clean thoroughly with degreaser
5. Dispose of materials properly

### Burn Injury

1. Move away from fryer
2. Cool burn under running water for 10+ minutes
3. Do NOT apply ice, butter, or ointments
4. Seek medical attention for serious burns
5. Report incident to manager

## End of Day Procedures

### Filtering

1. Turn off fryer and allow to cool to 250¬∞F
2. Position filter machine or pot
3. Drain oil through filter
4. Clean fryer interior
5. Return filtered oil to fryer
6. Cover fryer overnight

### Shutdown

1. Turn fryer OFF
2. Cover oil when cooled
3. Clean exterior surfaces
4. Mop area around fryer
5. Document oil quality in log
