# Alamo Prime Steakhouse — Wine List

> **15 wines** across 4 categories. Each category includes value, mid-range, and premium selections — the kind of list a restaurateur expects to see at a serious steakhouse.

---

## Red Wines (7)

Steakhouse reds — bold enough to stand up to Prime beef.

| # | Wine | Region | Varietal | Glass | Bottle | Tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **14 Hands Hot to Trot** | Washington State | Red Blend | $12 | $44 | Value |
| 2 | **Meiomi Pinot Noir** | California | Pinot Noir | $14 | $52 | Value |
| 3 | **Josh Cellars Cabernet Sauvignon** | California | Cabernet Sauvignon | $13 | $48 | Value |
| 4 | **Jordan Cabernet Sauvignon** | Alexander Valley, Sonoma | Cabernet Sauvignon | $24 | $90 | Mid |
| 5 | **Stags' Leap Artemis Cabernet Sauvignon** | Napa Valley | Cabernet Sauvignon | $30 | $115 | Mid |
| 6 | **Caymus Cabernet Sauvignon** | Napa Valley | Cabernet Sauvignon | — | $160 | Premium |
| 7 | **Silver Oak Cabernet Sauvignon** | Alexander Valley | Cabernet Sauvignon | — | $140 | Premium |

### Tasting Notes

- **14 Hands Hot to Trot** — Approachable everyday red. Ripe dark fruit, soft tannins, smooth finish. Crowd-pleaser for guests who want red wine without the sticker shock.
- **Meiomi Pinot Noir** — Rich and velvety for a Pinot. Dark cherry, mocha, vanilla. One of the most recognized Pinot Noir brands in the US — guests will know this label.
- **Josh Cellars Cabernet** — Dark fruit, vanilla, toasted oak. Massively popular by-the-glass steakhouse pour. Easy sell, reliable quality.
- **Jordan Cabernet** — Elegant Bordeaux-style Cab. Black cherry, cassis, subtle oak. A classic steakhouse list staple — the name alone carries weight with wine-savvy guests.
- **Stags' Leap Artemis** — Concentrated dark fruit, fine-grained tannins, long finish. Napa prestige without the Opus One price tag. Excellent with ribeye.
- **Caymus** — Lush, ripe, full-bodied Napa Cab. Dark cherry, cocoa, cassis. Iconic label — one of the most requested premium wines in American steakhouses. Bottle only.
- **Silver Oak** — Velvety and polished. Dark fruit, vanilla, baking spice from American oak aging. A celebratory bottle that guests recognize and trust. Bottle only.

### Tasting Notes

- **Kim Crawford** — Crisp, zesty, tropical fruit with citrus finish. The best-selling Sauvignon Blanc in the US — every guest knows it. Perfect with the Shrimp Cocktail.
- **Sonoma-Cutrer** — Balanced Chardonnay with green apple, pear, and a touch of oak. Not too buttery, not too lean. A reliable mid-range pour.
- **Cakebread Cellars** — Rich, layered Chardonnay. Citrus, stone fruit, baking spice, creamy texture. A Napa icon — guests order this by name.
- **Cloudy Bay** — Vibrant and aromatic. Passion fruit, grapefruit, herbaceous notes. Premium NZ Sauv Blanc with strong brand recognition.
