# Alamo Prime Steakhouse — Master Menu

> **Design principle:** Maximum prep sharing. The Herb Compound Butter and House Steak Seasoning cascade through steaks, sides, and one appetizer. Desserts share a single sauce. Total: **9 prep recipes** for a 10-item menu.

---

## Appetizers (3)

### 1. Classic Shrimp Cocktail
Chilled jumbo Gulf shrimp (5 ct), house-made cocktail sauce, lemon wedge, served over crushed ice in a martini glass.

### 2. Iceberg Wedge Salad
Quarter-head iceberg wedge, house blue cheese dressing, crumbled blue cheese, applewood-smoked bacon bits, grape tomatoes, fresh cracked pepper.

### 3. Seared Steak Bites
Tenderloin tips seared in cast iron with house steak seasoning, finished with herb compound butter, served on a sizzle plate with toothpicks.

---

## Entrees (5) — Steaks Only, Sides à la Carte

All steaks are seasoned with **House Steak Seasoning** and finished with **Herb Compound Butter**.

### 1. 14 oz Bone-In Ribeye
USDA Prime bone-in ribeye, seasoned and broiled, finished with a medallion of herb compound butter.

### 2. 8 oz Filet Mignon
Center-cut USDA Prime tenderloin, broiled to temperature, finished with herb compound butter.

### 3. 12 oz New York Strip
USDA Prime NY strip, seasoned and broiled, finished with herb compound butter.

### 4. 20 oz Bone-In Cowboy Cut
USDA Prime bone-in ribeye, thick-cut, seasoned and broiled, finished with herb compound butter.

### 5. 6 oz Petite Filet
USDA Prime tenderloin, broiled to temperature, finished with herb compound butter. Approachable price point.

---

## Sides (2) — à la Carte

### 1. Truffle Mashed Potatoes
Yukon Gold potatoes whipped with cream, butter, and truffle oil. Made in batch, held warm, portioned to order.

### 2. Creamed Spinach
Fresh spinach in a rich cream sauce, finished with a touch of nutmeg. Made in batch, held warm, portioned to order.

---

## Desserts (2) — Bakery Batch + Shared Sauce

Both desserts are baked in batch ahead of service and share the **Berry Compote** as their topping.

### 1. New York Cheesecake
Classic dense New York-style cheesecake on a graham cracker crust, topped with warm berry compote and a mint sprig.

### 2. Warm Brownie à la Mode
Rich dark chocolate brownie, warmed to order, topped with vanilla bean ice cream (purchased) and warm berry compote.

---
