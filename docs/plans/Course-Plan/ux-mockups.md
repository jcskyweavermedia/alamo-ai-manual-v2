## Device Priority

**iPad is the primary device.** Most training will happen on an iPad propped
up at a server station, in the break room, or at a two-top during a slow period.
Layouts are designed iPad-first, then adapted down to phone and up to desktop.

## Core Principles

### 1. Reuse Existing Product Viewers
When a section covers dishes, wines, cocktails, etc., we open the
**existing card views** (`DishCardView`, `WineCardView`, etc.).
No content interfaces are rebuilt.

### 2. New TrainingChatPanel (Purpose-Built for Teaching)
The AI teacher is NOT the existing Q&A chatbot. It's a new
`TrainingChatPanel` component built for conversational teaching:
- Leads the conversation (Socratic method)
- Remembers across sessions (persistent history)
- Tracks topic coverage (progress strip)
- Offers suggested replies (chips)
- Triggers evaluations (after enough interaction)
- Integrates with quizzes ("Ready for the quiz?")

### 3. Reused Building Blocks
The new panel reuses existing low-level components:
